How Can I Help?

Many people wonder how to keep the Earth healthy but don't know how to help. Don't worry, Saving Earth is here to help you protect the planet we all call home.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Much of the waste we throw away ends up in the oceans and harms the organisms living there. You may not think that this impacts you, but it surely does in way or another. The trash that we throw out can end up in the systems of fish and seafood that we eat and it can harm us too. One way to tackle this problem is to make less waste. Reducing waste means producing less of it. The lower the amount of waste you make, the less there is to reuse and recycle. One way to reduce is to use multi-purpose items. If the possibility arouses, buy multi-functional items. Another way to help is to think about if what you are using is something that you really need. Try not to make more waste than is absolutley necessary. It is very important to reuse or re-purpose items that have out-worn their intended use to restrict the amount of waste being thrown out.You can also help by donating these items. The process of recycling is turning an item into a raw material that can then be made into a new item which reduces waste on the planet.

Reduce

  1. Print on both sides of the paper
  2. Send mail electronically instead of on paper to reduce the amount of paper waste
  3. Cancel unwanted mail from mailing lists or magazines
  4. Try to buy items in person and without packaging because extra packaging becomes waste
  5. Refrain from using plastic straws and non-reusable cups
  6. Use washable napkins instead of paper napkins

Reuse

  1. Jars and pots can be used in the kitchen to hold items
  2. Reusable bags can hold groceries, other items, and reduce waste formed by plastic bags
  3. Newspaper can be used to pack items when transporting them
  4. Old books can be donated to a public library near you
  5. Used clothes can be donated to charity or thrift stores
  6. Rechargable batteries can be used multiple times and help reduce the wastage caused by regular batteries

Recycle

  1. Buy environmentaly friendly products (items that are made from recycled materials)
  2. Buy products that can be recycled like glass items
  3. Put products that can be recycled into recycaling bins, not the trash
  4. Research how your trash and recycling is being disposed of and find a new method if it is harming the environment
  5. Make a compost bin in your yard and dispose of organic matter there thayt can be turned into soil for a garden
  6. Make your own household cleaners
